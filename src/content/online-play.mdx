# How to use the app

## First steps

<Callout type="warning">
  We've been seeing performance problems with Safari, consider using a different browser.
</Callout>

1. Sign up to the [Blood on the Clocktower app](https://botc.app). It's free.
2. Log in.
3. Allow for camera/microphone access.
4. Press `[Q]` to open settings ‚Üí go to **Chat**.
5. Select your microphone/camera device from the respective dropdowns (note that they look like headers).

## Taking your notes

Treat the Grimoire as your personal notebook. Use it to track:

- Info you receive
- Characters others share with you
- Any theories or observations

Only you can see what you write.

### Private chats

During the day, invite anyone to a private voice chat:

- Press _Request Private Chat_ (or `[Enter]`)
- Click the üí¨ icon on their token

They'll get an invite ‚Äî no one else can join unless invited.

Leave a chat by:

- Pressing the exit icon in the _Voice_ panel
- Or pressing `[Backspace]`
